About the Accounting Certificate

Open a new door in your accounting career and acquire knowledge and skills you can use in companies large and small, government offices or the nonprofit sector. The demand for accountants is rising in all types of organizations and industries. Add a new professional designation to your title, keep up on current trends, and challenge yourself to be the best in your field.

Career Opportunities

Auditor

Prepare and examine financial records accurately and that taxes are paid properly and on time.

Average Median Pay $69,350

Accountant

Report financial statements truly and accurately and according to GAAP principles and laws.

Average Median Pay $78,750

Budget Analyst

Helps various organizations and businesses organize and allocate their financial resources.

Average Median Pay $98,500

Financial Manager

Produce financial reports, direct investments, and develop strategies for long-term financial goals.

Average Median Pay $104,000
